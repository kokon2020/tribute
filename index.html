Hanyu Yuzuru

Yuzuru Hanyu is a Japanese skater who competes in the men's singles discipline. He was born on December, 7, 1994 in Sendai, Miyagi, japan. Hanyu began figure skating when he was four years old. He became serious about pursuing the sport competitively after watching on television the heavily hyped duel between Russian skaters Aleksey Yagudin and Yevgeny Plushchenko at the 2002 Winter Olympics in Salt Lake City, Utah. He is a two-time Olympic champion (2014, 2018), a two-time World champion (2014, 2017), a four-time Grand Prix Final champion (2013–2016), a three-time Four Continents silver medalist (2011, 2013, 2017), the 2010 World Junior champion, the 2009–10 Junior Grand Prix Final champion, and a four-time Japanese national champion (2012–2015).
